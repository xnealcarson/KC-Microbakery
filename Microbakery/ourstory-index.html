Our Story

Rooted in tradition and baked with love, K.C. Microbakery is a Black-owned microbakery bringing handcrafted, small-batch goodness to the heart of the San Francisco Bay Area. What started as a passion project in a tiny home kitchen has grown into a community-driven bakery dedicated to fresh, high-quality ingredients and time-honored baking techniques. Every loaf, pastry, and treat we create reflects the warmth of home and the rich culinary heritage passed down through generations.

We believe that food has the power to connect people, and that's why we pour our heart into every bite. Whether it's our signature sourdough, buttery croissants, or indulgent sweet rolls, our goal is to provide a taste of comfort and joy. We proudly source from local farmers and businesses, ensuring that each creation supports the community that has uplifted us. More than just a bakery, K.C. Microbakery is a place where culture, flavor, and craftsmanship come together—one delicious bite at a time.

Our Baker

Kerstin Carson, the heart and hands behind K.C. Microbakery, is a Bay Area native with deep roots in East Palo Alto. A former English teacher, Kerstin has always been passionate about nurturing her community—first through education and now through baking. What began as a creative outlet in her home kitchen quickly became a calling, blending her love for storytelling with the artistry of handcrafted bread and pastries. She believes that, much like a good book, great food has the power to bring people together, spark conversations, and create lasting memories.

More than just a baker, Kerstin is committed to giving back to the community that raised her. She sources ingredients from local farmers, collaborates with neighborhood organizations, and donates fresh-baked goods to those in need. Whether she’s perfecting a sourdough recipe or mentoring aspiring bakers, her goal is simple: to spread warmth, joy, and a sense of belonging—one loaf at a time. K.C. Microbakery isn’t just a business; it’s a love letter to her hometown, a place where tradition meets innovation, and where every bite tells a story.
